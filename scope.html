Scope

Our bug bounty program focuses on the following areas:

  • Website Frontend
  • APIs
  • User Authentication
  • Data Storage and Retrieval
  • Scoping of BBPs The feature descriptions pertaining to scoping of BBPs are enumerated as follows.
  • • Single product and current ver- sion: BBP scoped to test current version of products.
  • • Old and new interdependencies: Scope includes testing the prod- uct interdependencies with old and new product versions.
  • • Third-party interdependencies: Scope includes testing inter- dependencies with third-party products.
  • • Acquired firm interdependen- cies: Scope includes testing interdependencies with acquired products.
  • • Generic products: Scope in- cludes all of the firm’s products and commissioned versions.

Testing outside the specified scope is not encouraged, and we kindly request that you focus your efforts on the listed components to ensure a smoother review process.
