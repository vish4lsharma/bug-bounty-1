Response Time

We take the security of our platform seriously and strive to respond to vulnerability reports promptly. Our target response times are as follows:

  • Critical and High Severity: Within 24 hours
  • Medium Severity: Within 3 business days
  • Low Severity: Within 5 business days

Please note that these are target response times, and actual response times may vary based on the complexity of the reported issue and other factors. We appreciate your patience as we work to address and resolve security vulnerabilities.
