Rules of Engagement

Thank you for participating in our bug bounty program. To ensure a smooth collaboration, please adhere to the following rules:

  1. Respect the Scope: Only test within the specified scope mentioned in our program details.
  2. Responsible Disclosure: Report vulnerabilities promptly and responsibly.
  3. No Data Manipulation: Do not modify or delete user data during testing.
  4. No Social Engineering: Avoid phishing, social engineering, or any form of physical attacks.
  5. No Denial of Service: Do not attempt to disrupt our services.
  6. No Violation of Privacy: Respect user privacy and confidentiality.
  7. Provide Detailed Reports: Include clear and detailed reports of identified vulnerabilities.
  8. Do Not Publicly Disclose: Refrain from publicly disclosing vulnerabilities until we provide consent.
  9. No Exploitation for Personal Gain: Do not exploit any vulnerability for personal gain.
  10. Follow the Law: Ensure your actions comply with applicable laws and regulations.

Failure to comply with these rules may result in disqualification from the bug bounty program. We appreciate your cooperation in making our platform more secure.
