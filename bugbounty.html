Rewards

Our Bug Bounty Program offers generous rewards for security researchers who responsibly disclose vulnerabilities. Help us make our system more secure, and you may be eligible for the following rewards:

  • Cash rewards
  • Recognition in our Security Hall of Fame
  • Swag and merchandise

Scope

Our Bug Bounty Program focuses on the following areas:

  • Web applications
  • Mobile applications
  • APIs

Rules of Engagement

Participating in our Bug Bounty Program comes with certain rules to ensure a safe and ethical environment for everyone involved. Please review the following rules before submitting vulnerabilities:

  • Do not disclose vulnerabilities publicly before resolution
  • Do not exploit vulnerabilities for malicious purposes
  • Respect the privacy and integrity of our users' data
