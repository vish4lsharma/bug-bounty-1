Disclosure Policy

Our disclosure policy is designed to ensure transparent communication regarding security vulnerabilities. Please adhere to the following guidelines when reporting vulnerabilities:

  1. Report the vulnerability promptly after discovery.
  2. Provide detailed information on the identified issue, including steps to reproduce.
  3. Avoid public disclosure until we provide consent.
  4. Respect the privacy and confidentiality of our users' data.
  5. Follow responsible disclosure practices within the scope of our bug bounty program.

By participating in our bug bounty program, you agree to abide by this disclosure policy. We appreciate your cooperation in maintaining the security of our platform.
